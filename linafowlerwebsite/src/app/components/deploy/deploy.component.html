Deployment Dashboard

Problem: Scrum Teams needed a way to validate each release of their monolith application to ensure teams did not have failing tests or bugs that would effect other parts of the application before being deployed to production.

Below is the process of how we reached a solution to the problem above and how the Deployment Dashboard came to life.

Strategy

To begin the design process I started with empathizing with our users. Before we began work on the project we needed to determine the problem we were trying to solve.
